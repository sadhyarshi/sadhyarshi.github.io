About Me

I am a passionate and innovative Developer and Creative Artist with a strong background in both software development and digital illustration. With expertise in full-stack web development, I have honed my skills in crafting functional and aesthetically pleasing websites. My creative journey also extends to the world of vector illustration, where I bring ideas to life through vibrant and detailed artworks.

As a software developer, I specialize in Java and Spring frameworks, leveraging these technologies to build scalable, robust, and efficient web applications. My technical toolkit also includes React, HTML, CSS, and SQL, which I use to create seamless and user-friendly interfaces. My coding philosophy revolves around writing clean, maintainable code that balances functionality with performance.

My dual expertise allows me to merge technical precision with artistic expression, creating digital experiences that are not only technically sound but also visually compelling. Whether it's developing dynamic web applications or designing intricate vector graphics, I am committed to excellence in every project I undertake.
